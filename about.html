Founder Bio

Rezulie Ventures is led by Alireza Shafaei, a Machine Learning expert with a Ph.D. in Machine Learning & Computer Vision from the University of British Columbia (UBC).

LinkedIn Profile

Professional Background

Prior to founding Rezulie Ventures, Alireza was the co-founder and CTO of Skylab, a computer vision startup that was successfully acquired by Imagequix. During his tenure, he led the technical team in developing cutting-edge ML solutions for real-world applications.

With experience spanning both academic research and industry leadership, Alireza brings a unique perspective that combines theoretical depth with practical implementation knowledge.

Consulting Philosophy

"I work closely with technical founders and teams to ship high-quality ML products fast. My goal is to bring production thinking to the earliest stages of product design."

This philosophy drives every engagement at Rezulie Ventures. We believe that even early-stage ML products should be built with production-readiness in mind, avoiding the common pitfalls that lead to technical debt and scaling challenges later.
