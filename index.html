About Rezulie Ventures

Rezulie Ventures is led by Alireza Shafaei, a Machine Learning expert with a Ph.D. in Machine Learning & Computer Vision from the University of British Columbia (UBC).

Prior to founding Rezulie Ventures, Alireza was the co-founder and CTO of Skylab, a computer vision startup that was successfully acquired.

Our mission is to help startups build production-ready ML systems that deliver real business value and scale with your growth.
